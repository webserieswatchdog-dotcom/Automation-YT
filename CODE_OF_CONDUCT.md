# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Focus on collaboration and learning.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Spam or malicious contributions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation that violates this code.
